Previous and current research projects

Plus some photos I have taken while researching plant-animal interactions.

Several species of leaftiers on Quercus species in Missouri, USA

Leaftying micro-lepidopterans are an important ecosystem engineer in herbivore/detritivore relationships that are common on temperate decidious trees. They have been shown to increase the abundance of certain species/guilds of insects that would not be there if not for the leaftie. My master's presentation.
